Welcome to SAGA

We are studying a sample of "Galactic Analogs", galaxies in the SDSS that are selected to be like the Milky Way. In particular, we are interested in the satellite galaxies belonging to these hosts. Is the Milky Way's satellite population unusual? How do Milky Way-like galaxies compare with predictions from simulations with regard to their satellites?
